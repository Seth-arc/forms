Welcome to AidData Training

Traditional training methods such as lectures, presentations, and theoretical discussions have their place. But when it comes to preparing professionals to handle the interconnected challenges of data-intensive work environments, they often fall short. Real-world problems rarely arrive neatly packaged with clear solutions. Instead, they involve multiple stakeholders, competing priorities, and high levels of uncertainty.

Our Approach

Data-to-Policy Curriculum

AidData data and tools are incorporated to empower policymakers, analysts, and journalists with the skills to transform data into insights and actionable decisions

John Custer, AidData: Deputy Director, Communications and Data Analytics leading a development finance data workshop.

Innovative Strategies

Multimodal and technology-based delivery are used to ensure learners can effectively interpret data and implement solutions in real-world scenarios.

Sethu Nguna, AidData: Manager, Training and Instructional Design Manager leading a data journalism simulation session.

Beyond Traditional Training

AidData employs a hybrid model that combines foundational online courses, delivered in partnership with the William & Mary's Studio for Teaching and Learning Innovation, with engaging deep-dive workshops and practical online simulations led by AidData subject matter experts.
